Mesmerizing Ratargul Swamp Forest!

Located in Sylhet,the only swamp forest of Bangladesh.

Government of Bangladesh is conserving this beautiful piece of beauty in our country.

Swamp forests are specific forests overwhelmed with fresh water,permanently or seasonaly.This swamp forest is rich with fresh water and situated by the river Goain and linked with the Channel Chengir khal.
